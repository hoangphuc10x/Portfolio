Scan-to-order — table QR opens the menu

html5-qrcode · RequireTable guard

Each table's pre("save") hook builds a slug and a QR encoding ${URL_CLIENT}/${slug}/check (uploaded to Cloudinary). A guest scans it with Html5Qrcode; CheckPage resolves the table, saves it to Redux, and the ordering routes stay locked behind RequireTable until a table is set.

Kitchen queue & live order lifecycle

Socket.IO · KitchenQueue · FoodStatus

On submit, OrderService.createOrUpdateOrder() writes each item into the KitchenQueue collection. The chef-head assigns a chef; chefs advance each item's FoodStatus; the order's TableOrderStatus transitions in step. Every change is broadcast over Socket.IO and clients refetch.

Monorepo: client/ (React + Vite SPA) talks to server/ (Express + TypeScript) over REST (RTK Query, Bearer JWT with silent refresh) and Socket.IO. The server persists to MongoDB via Mongoose, keeps a Redis (ioredis) client, and offloads images & table QR codes to Cloudinary. Built & deployed by GitHub Actions to Render (server) and Vercel (client).
